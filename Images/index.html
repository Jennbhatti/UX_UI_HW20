A mobile Collaborative app that aims to make neighborhoods more connected. We want to provide users with a way to request or offer services to their community

In this research project, I planned and conducted 2 rounds of interviews with potential users as well as interviews constructed a google survey. From there I designed the section that allows users to offer and request services, as well as the pages where the services are listed on. I was also responsible for the construction and design of the community page. Through usability testing I also iterated the navigation bar.

Problem

After conducting User interviews, we transcribed the notes and then worked together to group the sticky notes. The sticky notes were then labeled, in doing such we were able to find the similarities that users had as well as the major pain points

-Paper prototype of the landing pages for the services and requests pages and the calendar page. The calendar would a shared calendar with events that the residents of the area could plan. -This low-fidelity clickable prototype was used for initial testing of our app.
